Burger Recipe

Description

A basic, delicious burger, made with a soft, lightly toasted bun, juicy meat, topped with melted cheese and finished with a layer of mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Grill the meat
  2. Melt the cheese
  3. Toast the bread
  4. Assemble the burger
