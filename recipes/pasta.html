Pasta Recipe

Description

Simple, tasty pasta, made with al dente noodles, rich tomato sauce, topped with grated cheese and finished with a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Fresh Herbs

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Heat the sauce
  3. Add the grated cheese
  4. Finish with herbs
